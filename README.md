S – Situation

HR teams often struggle to monitor critical workforce metrics such as employee attrition, hiring trends, performance distribution, and demographics. Without a clear visualization platform, decision-making becomes slow and reactive.

T – Task

I aimed to build a clean, interactive Power BI HR Analytics Dashboard that provides HR managers with clear insights into KPIs and enables data-driven workforce planning.

A – Action

Cleaned, transformed, and modeled the HR dataset using Power Query.

Created DAX measures for KPIs like total employees, attrition rate, average salary, and department-wise headcount.

Designed interactive visuals such as:

KPI Cards (Employee Count, Attrition %, Avg Salary, Hiring Rate)

Department-wise Attrition & Performance Charts

Gender Distribution

Job Role & Education Insights

Age Group & Tenure Analysis

Added slicers for department, role, education, and gender to enable drill-down exploration.

Applied a clean and intuitive Power BI layout focused on usability and HR decision-making.

R – Result

Delivered a dashboard that gives HR stakeholders instant visibility into workforce issues and trends.

Enabled faster decision-making on attrition, hiring needs, and performance gaps.

Received positive feedback for professional design, accurate DAX metrics, and dashboard clarity.

Strengthened my skills in Power BI, DAX, data modeling, and business-driven analytics.
